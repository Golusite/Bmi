BMI Kya Hai?

Body Mass Index (BMI) ek simple calculation hai jo tumhare weight aur height ka use karke batata hai ki kya tumhare sharir ka utna weight hai jo tumhari height ke hisab sahi hai.

Indian Standards

Indians ko higher health risk hota hai kam BMI par bhi. Isliye Indian standards thode strict hain:

  • Underweight: Less than 18.5
  • Normal: 18.5 - 22.9
  • Overweight: 23.0 - 24.9
  • Obese: 25.0 or more
